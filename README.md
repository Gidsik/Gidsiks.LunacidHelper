# Gidsiks.LunacidHelper

Simple web app that helps you play Lunacid by showing you impact of **Lunacy** stat on your gameplay.

Can show:
- Lunacy gain by casting a spell based on todays moon phase, spell cost and your maximum MP
- Spell DMG multiplier based on your current Lunacy
- XP multiplier based on your current Lunacy and todays moon phase
- Table with "Forecast" in selected date range showing moon phases and it's impact on lunacy gain and xp bonus
